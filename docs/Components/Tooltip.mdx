<p>Tooltip provides on-demand, contextual information to users as they hover over or focus on elements, enhancing understanding and interaction without cluttering the interface.</p>

  </tr>

    <td>isDark</td>

  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>hidePointer</td>
    <td>boolean</td>
    <td>false</td>
    <td>Hides the Tooltip pointer</td>
  </tr>
